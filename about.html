About

I'm currently working at Island Photography, polishing my organizational tendencies and leadership skills.

On Monday nights, I compete in a social sport league known as LI Kick where I've played kickball for three seasons.

From my time in school at NYIT I've learned many fundamentals of software development and have been very interested in the processes since. I've always gravitated towards the aspects of frontend development, since it aligns more with my hobbies in the arts.

I've always been a fast learner able to pick up new skills, especially when they align with my interests.
